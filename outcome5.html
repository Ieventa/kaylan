Contributing Knowledge

Students will be able to draw conclusions based on analysis and interpretation of primary evidence and place that work in conversation with other source materials.

Final Research Paper

Digging into the emotional complexities of AI companionship in my final project helped me realize that contributing knowledge isn’t just about saying something new—it’s about adding meaningfully to an ongoing conversation. By analyzing real user experiences and coding qualitative data from platforms like Reddit and app reviews, I was able to uncover emotional patterns that don’t always show up in mainstream AI discussions. Instead of just repeating what scholars like Pan & Mou or Liu-Thompkins said, I connected their work to firsthand data and expanded on it with my own insights about authenticity, dependence, and digital intimacy. This assignment showed me that my voice has a place in academic discourse when it’s backed by thoughtful research, ethical analysis, and a genuine attempt to understand human behavior in the digital age.

It wasn’t just about learning—it was about contributing fresh insights into an evolving field.
