About This Portfolio

Hi! I'm Kayla Nguyen. This portfolio is a collection of my work from ENC 1102, where I explored composition, rhetoric, and critical thinking across a bunch of different formats and genres.

Each piece reflects growth in my writing process, my ability to engage with sources, and how I shape my voice to suit different rhetorical situations. I also wanted this site to feel like *me*, so there's a mix of professionalism and personality throughout.
